Summer Watermelon Gazpacho

Description

A delicious summer treat to share with the entire family

Ingredients

  • Seeded watermelon
  • Roma tomatoes
  • Red bell pepper
  • English cucumber
  • Minced shallot
  • Fresh lime juice
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Feta cheese
  • Fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a high-powered blender
  2. Stir gazpacho before serving
